VISITOR POLICY
TechHub Lagos Office

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PURPOSE

This Visitor Policy outlines the rules and procedures for admitting, monitoring, and managing visitors to the TechHub Lagos office in order to maintain a safe, secure, and professional environment for staff, clients, and guests.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS

- All visitors must present a valid government-issued ID (National ID, Driver’s Licence, Voter’s Card, or International Passport) at reception.

- Visitors must be pre-registered by a staff member at least 2 hours before arrival, except in urgent situations approved by a manager.

- The reception/registration desk operates Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

- Walk-in visitors (not pre-registered) will be asked to wait while their host is contacted for approval. If the host cannot be reached within 15 minutes, entry may be denied.

- Each visitor must sign the visitor log, providing full name, organisation, phone number, host name, and time of arrival.

- Maximum visit duration without extension is 4 hours. Longer visits require approval from the host’s line manager.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PARKING INFORMATION

- Visitor parking is available in Lot B on the ground floor (east side of the building).

- Parking is free for the first 2 hours.

- After the first 2 hours, parking is charged at ₦500 per additional hour.

- Disabled parking spaces are available near the main entrance and are free of charge for vehicles displaying appropriate permits.

- Motorcycle parking is available in the designated area near the security post; motorcycles should not be parked in car bays.

- Visitors must collect a parking ticket on entry and present it at reception for validation before exiting the premises.

- Vehicles parked in no-parking zones, fire lanes, or blocking access routes may be towed at the owner’s expense.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CHECK-IN AND BADGES

- Upon successful registration, visitors will receive a temporary visitor badge.

- Badges must be worn visibly at all times while on the premises.

- Visitor badges are colour-coded to indicate access level (e.g., “Guest”, “Vendor”, “Interviewee”).

- Badges are valid only for the date of issue and expire automatically at 11:59 PM.

- Visitors must return their badges to reception when leaving the building and sign out with the actual time of departure.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECURITY PROTOCOLS

- Visitors are only permitted in designated areas relevant to their visit and must follow all instructions from security and staff.

- Visitors are not allowed access to floors 5–7, which include executive offices and the data centre, unless explicitly authorised and accompanied by an authorised staff member.

- All visitors must be escorted by their host when entering restricted areas or meeting rooms that contain sensitive information or equipment.

- Bags, laptops, and other personal belongings may be subject to screening at the discretion of Security.

- Photography, audio recording, or video recording inside the building requires prior written approval from the Admin or Communications team.

- In case of an emergency (such as fire alarm or evacuation), visitors must follow the directions of staff and Security and assemble at the designated assembly point in Parking Lot A.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VISITOR CONDUCT

- Visitors are expected to behave in a professional and respectful manner towards staff, other visitors, and property.

- Aggressive behaviour, harassment, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated and may result in immediate removal from the premises.

- Smoking and vaping are prohibited inside the building. A designated smoking area is available outside near the security post.

- Visitors must not interfere with the work of employees or access unattended workstations or documents.

- Visitors should use only the guest WiFi network and must not attempt to connect to internal networks or plug devices into company systems without IT approval.

- Food and drinks are permitted only in the cafeteria and designated break areas, not in server rooms, conference rooms with sensitive equipment, or open-plan workspaces unless expressly allowed.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CHILDREN AND SPECIAL GUESTS

- Children under 16 must be accompanied and supervised by an adult host at all times.

- School or group tours must be scheduled in advance and are subject to capacity and security approval.

- VIP or high-profile visitors may have customised security and access arrangements approved by Management and Security.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CONFIDENTIALITY

- Visitors may be required to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before attending meetings where confidential information is discussed.

- Documents, whiteboards, and screens containing sensitive information should be cleared or concealed before visitors enter meeting areas.

- Visitors must not photograph or copy internal documents or displays without explicit permission.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BREACHES AND ENFORCEMENT

- Any breach of this Visitor Policy may lead to immediate removal from the premises.

- Staff are responsible for ensuring their guests comply with this policy. Hosts may be held accountable for repeated or serious violations by their visitors.

- Serious incidents may be reported to law enforcement or other relevant authorities.
