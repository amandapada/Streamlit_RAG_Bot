SECURITY GUIDELINES
TechHub Lagos Office

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES

Fire Emergency:
- Upon hearing the fire alarm, evacuate immediately using the nearest stairwell.
- DO NOT use elevators during emergencies.
- Proceed to the designated assembly point in Parking Lot A.
- Wait for clearance from the Emergency Response Team before re-entering the building.
- Fire extinguishers are located near each stairwell on every floor.

Medical Emergency:
- Contact Security immediately at extension 999 or dial 0 from any desk phone.
- First aid kits are available at reception and on each floor near the restrooms.
- Trained first aiders are identified by badges and listed on notice boards.
- For life-threatening emergencies, Security will contact Lagos State Emergency (112).

Security Threat:
- Report any suspicious activity, persons, or items to Security immediately.
- Follow all instructions issued by Security personnel or management.
- Lockdown or shelter-in-place procedures will be announced via the public address (PA) system.
- Emergency contact email: security@techhub.com.ng.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BUILDING ACCESS CONTROL

Staff Access:
- Main entrance uses biometric (fingerprint) access for all staff, available 24/7.
- Access cards and credentials must not be shared or loaned to anyone.
- Staff may enter the building outside regular office hours using their access card.
- Malfunctioning access cards or biometric issues must be reported to IT Support.

Visitor Access:
- All visitors must enter through the main reception only.
- Visitors must sign in, present a valid ID, and wear a visible visitor badge at all times.
- After 6:00 PM, all visitors must be escorted by their staff host.
- Weekend visitors require written pre-approval from the relevant department head.

Restricted Areas:
- Server Room (Floor 6): IT staff only; biometric plus PIN required.
- Executive Floor (Floor 7): Executives and authorized personnel only.
- Loading Bay: Facilities staff and approved vendors only.
- Any unauthorized access attempt will be logged and may result in disciplinary action.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LOST AND FOUND

- Lost items should be reported to the Security desk on the ground floor.
- Found items must be handed over to Security as soon as possible.
- Items are held for 30 days before disposal or donation.
- To claim an item, you must present a valid ID and accurately describe the item.
- Contact: security@techhub.com.ng or extension 777 for enquiries.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DATA AND DEVICE SECURITY

- Always lock your workstation when away from your desk (Windows + L or Ctrl + Cmd + Q on Mac).
- Do not leave laptops, phones, or personal valuables unattended in open areas.
- Confidential or sensitive documents must be stored in locked cabinets or shredded after use.
- USB drives and external storage devices must be scanned and approved by IT before use on company systems.
- Lost or stolen company devices (laptops, phones, tokens) must be reported immediately to IT and Security.
- Avoid viewing sensitive information on screens in public or crowded areas; use privacy filters where necessary.
- Never share passwords, one-time codes, or multi-factor authentication tokens with anyone.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CCTV AND MONITORING

- CCTV cameras operate 24/7 in common areas, entrances, corridors, and parking lots.
- Video footage is retained for 90 days for security and investigative purposes.
- No cameras are installed in restrooms, prayer rooms, or private offices to respect privacy.
- Requests to review footage must be approved by the Security Manager and HR.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VENDOR AND CONTRACTOR SECURITY

- All vendors and contractors must sign in at reception and wear visitor or contractor badges.
- Vendors working after hours must be pre-approved by Facilities and Security.
- Vendors must be accompanied by a staff member in restricted or sensitive areas.
- Removal of equipment, furniture, or IT hardware from the building requires a signed gate-pass and Security clearance.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

INCIDENT REPORTING

- All security incidents (e.g., theft, harassment, threats, vandalism, unauthorized access) must be reported to Security within 24 hours.
- Anonymous reporting is available via security@techhub.com.ng with “CONFIDENTIAL” in the subject line.
- All reported incidents are logged, investigated, and escalated where necessary.
- Retaliation against staff who report incidents in good faith is strictly prohibited.
